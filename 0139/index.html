terms#

  • PANI: Polyaniline.
  • FD: Finite Difference.
  • DR1: Disperse Red 1.
  • MNDO: Modified Neglect of Diatomic Overlap.
  • CI: Configuration Interaction
  • QM: Quantum Mechanics.
  • MD: Molecular Dynamics.
  • ABM: Agent-Based Model.
  • SD: System Dynamics.
